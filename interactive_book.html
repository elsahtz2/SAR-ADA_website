Pearson Correlation

The Pearson correlation will be used systematically on all the features, allowing us to extract a first glimpse of which of them is significant on the rating difference. The Pearson correlation measures the linear correlation between two sets of data, so by measuring the Pearson Correlation Coefficient (PCC) between one feature and the rating difference, we will extract the linear correlation between both. The significance threshold has been chosen at the p-value of 0.05 since it's the most commonly used value.
